About Me

My name is Hollund, and I am above average interested in growing vegetables and plants. Not only do the vegetables taste good, but they give an incredible feeling of mastery.

My plant journey started out with tomatoes. I did not put much effort at first, but they rewarded me with lots of tomatoes, which resulted in a new hobby.

I live in Bergen, which presents some challenges when it comes to cultivation. Bergen is known for being the wettest city in Norway, a fact that is very much true. Even though it pours most days, it is possible to grow lots of vegetables.

In this blog I will share how I am able to grow plants both on a small balcony and with a big garden. I hope you will follow my blog.
